About Me

Hi! I'm Ridho Tri Pangestu, a passionate UI/UX Designer and Frontend Developer based in Indonesia.


I'm currently a second-semester Information Systems student at UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta, where I've developed a strong interest in creating intuitive and visually engaging digital experiences.


I believe great design is where aesthetics meet functionality, and I'm dedicated to crafting user interfaces that are both beautiful and user-friendly. My focus is on modern web design, responsive layouts, and interactive experiences that make a lasting impact. I enjoy the entire process—from wireframing and prototyping to bringing designs to life through code. Right now, I'm improving my skills in:

  • >Figma for UI/UX design and prototyping
  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React for frontend development
  • Building personal projects and exploring emerging web technologies to continuously grow my portfolio

I'm always eager to learn, collaborate, and take on new challenges that push my creative and technical boundaries.
